Medical Disclaimer

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Not Medical Advice

PrEPPED+ does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The app is a personal organizational and informational tool designed to help you track PrEP medication adherence, sexual health activities, STI testing, and vaccine records.

Informational Content

PrEPPED+ includes educational articles and information about STIs, PrEP, sexual health, and related topics. This content is provided for general informational purposes only and is not intended to replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date health information sourced from reputable organizations such as the CDC, WHO, and peer-reviewed research, medical knowledge evolves and individual circumstances vary. The information in PrEPPED+ may not reflect the most current research or apply to your specific situation.

Protection Status

PrEPPED+ may display indicators related to protection status (e.g., whether an encounter was logged as "protected"). These indicators reflect only what you have entered into the app. They do not constitute a medical assessment of your risk level and should not be relied upon to determine your actual level of protection against STIs or HIV.

Always Consult a Healthcare Provider

You should always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding:

  • Starting, stopping, or changing PrEP or any medication
  • Interpreting STI test results or symptoms
  • Treatment options after a positive test result
  • Post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP) decisions
  • Vaccine scheduling, eligibility, and side effects
  • Any health concerns related to sexual activity

Do not delay seeking medical advice, disregard professional medical guidance, or discontinue medical treatment because of information presented in PrEPPED+.

Emergency Situations

If you believe you have a medical emergency, call your local emergency services immediately. PrEPPED+ is not designed for use in emergency situations and should never be used as a substitute for emergency medical care.

No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of PrEPPED+ does not create a doctor-patient relationship between you and PrEPPED+ or its creators. The app is a tool to help you organize your personal health information, not a healthcare provider.
