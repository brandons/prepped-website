Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

PrEPPED ("we", "our", or "the app") is a sexual health tracking application. We built PrEPPED with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and your rights.

1. Data Storage

All health data you enter into PrEPPED — including PrEP dose logs, encounter records, STI test results, vaccine records, and related notes — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your health data.

Your data is saved using Apple's on-device storage frameworks (SwiftData). It is not uploaded to any cloud service, and we have no ability to access it.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account credentials (PrEPPED has no accounts)
  • Health data (PrEP logs, encounters, test results, vaccine records)
  • Location data
  • Photos or documents you attach to test records
  • Partner information

3. Third-Party Services

PrEPPED uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — to manage in-app subscriptions (PrEPPED Pro). RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and purchase transaction data from Apple. It does not receive any health data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — handles payment processing for subscriptions. Apple's standard terms and privacy policy apply.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking pixels.

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders (PrEP dose reminders, testing reminders), these are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's notification system. Notification content is generated on-device and is not sent through our servers. If you enable "Discreet Notifications," reminder text is replaced with generic language.

5. Apple Watch & Widgets

If you use the PrEPPED Apple Watch app or home screen widgets, data is transferred between your iPhone and Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication happens directly between your paired devices and does not pass through any external server.

6. Siri Shortcuts

PrEPPED offers Siri Shortcuts for quick actions (logging a dose, checking your streak). These intents are processed on-device by iOS. We do not receive voice data or Siri interaction logs.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time from Settings > Delete All Data within the app. This permanently removes all encounters, tests, medication logs, and preferences from your device. Because we do not store your data remotely, deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

8. Biometric Security

PrEPPED supports Face ID / Touch ID to lock the app. Biometric data is handled entirely by Apple's LocalAuthentication framework. We never access, store, or transmit biometric data.

9. Children

PrEPPED is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at privacy@getpreppedapp.com.
